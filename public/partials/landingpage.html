Your personal dashboard

After subscribing to your the shows which you want to watch you regularly, the dashboard lists the latest and upcoming episodes with the information where and when to watch them. The dashboard shows also a short description of the episode if available, which is useful if you don't remember if you already watched an episode or not.

Find new TV shows

TvShowOrganizer helps you explore new shows. You can search for a show and list information for a show, like actors, network and an overview of every season and its episodes. You can also list shows for specific actors or networks.
