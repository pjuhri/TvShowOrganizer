Organize Your TV Shows

Never miss an episode of your favorite tv show! Tv Show Organizer allows you to subscribe to the shows you are watching and presents you a dashboard with the latest episodes so you are always up to date!

You may enter any email address you want, but you can only reset your password with a valid one.

TheTVDB

Your favourite show is missing? Head over to TheTVDB and help them update their database. On the next daily update your show will appear if you search for it.

Contributing

A feature is missing or you found a bug? Visit the project on github and open a ticket or send a pull request.

Mobile

This application was optimized for use on mobile phones. Just create a shortcut on your homescreen! Help on how to do so can be found here for Firefox and here for Chrome, but it will also work with most of the other mobile browsers.
